FAQs

Token Distribution

When will I receive my STORJ?

We understand that you’ve been anxiously awaiting the distribution of your STORJ and are excited to report that the STORJ token has officially been distributed!

The STORJ token contract address is: 0xb64ef51c888972c908cfacf59b47c1afbc0ab8ac

You will need to set your wallet to watch the token contract. While instructions vary by wallet and we always recommend referring to their official documentation, we have provided instructions for many of the most common ERC-20 wallets later in the FAQ.

Why did it take so long to distribute the STORJ token?

The Storj Token sale is unique in many ways:

- Because the Storj network has been running for over a year, there was a significant ecosystem that needed to be updated prior to the release of the new token.
- In addition to the STORj token distribution, our team also had to finalize the custom application to convert SJCX to STORJ.
- Additional time was required to implement timelock smart contracts for tokens sold during the pre-sale.


Some of the above tasks have never been done before in any other token sale. As a result, additional time and care were taken to ensure that the process was done correctly, every step of the way.

What do I have to do to see the STORJ token in my wallet?

This will depend largely on the Ethereum wallet that you used. For many, the STORJ token will appear automatically. If this does not occur, you will need to add the token manually to your wallet. Each wallet should have a well-documented process for this. For most major wallets, instructions can be found later in our FAQ.

Token Wallets & Management

How can I manually add the STORJ token to my wallet?

The STORJ token contract address is: 0xb64ef51c888972c908cfacf59b47c1afbc0ab8ac

Below, you will find instructions for most major ERC-20 wallets. For any not listed, we recommend reviewing the wallet provider’s official documentation.

MyEtherWallet
  • Navigate to the “Send Ether and Tokens” page.
  • Under ‘Token Balances’, select the option to ‘Add Custom Token’.
  • Enter the STORJ contract address in the “Address” field
  • Enter “STORJ” in the “Token Symbol” field
  • Enter “8” in the “Decimals” field


Parity
  • Enable the “Contracts” tab in your Parity Settings
  • Navigate to the Contracts tab and click “Watch”
  • On the “Choose a Contract Type” screen, select “Token” and click “Next”
  • Enter the STORJ contract address in the “network address” field
  • Enter “Storj Token” in the “contract name” field and click “Add Contract”


Jaxx
Jaxx will support STORJ tokens in their next update, so please hold tight and they will appear automatically after the update.

imToken
imToken will automatically recognize and show your STORJ tokens.

I did not supply a receiving address during the token sale. Where will my STORJ tokens go?

If you purchased STORJ tokens using BTC as your payment, you were asked to supply an Ethereum wallet address. This Ethereum wallet address is where the tokens will be delivered.

If you purchased STORJ tokens using ETH as your payment, then the tokens will be delivered to the same Ethereum wallet address that was used to make the purchase. In other words, your payment address is also your receiving address.

What is Storj?

Storj is a peer-to-peer network comprised of “farmers,” users who rent out their spare hard drive space and bandwidth, and "renters" who purchase space and bandwidth.

Why use a token?

We recently announced a migration of the Storj token, STORJ (formerly SJCX) to the Ethereum ERC20 standard from the Counterparty protocol on the Bitcoin blockchain. This move helps us reduce transaction fees for the Storj community while also increasing functionality and usability.

There are many benefits to using STORJ:

Granularity

Bitcoin isn’t divisible or flexible enough to support the granularity needed for micropayments.

Stability

A separate token like STORJ provides a layer of protection from the volatility of larger markets.

Scalability

Using STORJ helps us identify and fix problems at scale, paving the way for a larger, more stable network.

Incentivization

We pay for our community's hard work performing tasks like tier-one tech support and development with STORJ.

Micropayments

A network funded hub would allow for quick transactions, as well as keep transaction fees low for the users.

Partnerships

Storj Labs is dedicated to building long-lasting partnerships and supporting the broader [Ethereum] ecosystem.

Ecosystem

Users can earn STORJ for sharing disk space, then use it as renters themselves, creating a positive feedback loop.

Future Growth

We plan to use the token in ways raw Bitcoin or Ether can’t. For example, the Storj-specific signaling described in SIP0002.

Storj is designed to be flexible and modular.

We believe that using a token is the best payment system for the network.
